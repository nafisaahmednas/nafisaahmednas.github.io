Nafisa Ahmed

Research Assistant, Bachelor of Science in Biotechnology, BRAC University, Bangladesh

Hey! I’m Nafisa. I’m a recent Biotechnology Graduate at BRAC University and an advocate of accessible healthcare. I am a results-oriented Research Assistant with experience in experimental design, data collection, and a demonstrated ability to tackle complex scientific puzzles.

As an aspiring researcher, I delved into the fascinating world of DNA repair genes and their association with prostate cancer risk in the Bangladeshi population for my undergraduate research thesis. I even explored how these genetic variations correlated with the biochemical parameters of the patients. Excitingly, I presented my findings at the prestigious AACR Annual Meeting 2023. In recognition of my diligent efforts, I proudly received the prestigious "Special Recognition Award" from BRAC University. Besides this, I have been actively involved in scientific writing throughout my undergraduate years. If you're interested in my research projects, hop over to my Google Scholar page for more. Currently, I am working as a Research Assistant at BRAC University, overlooking students in in their assessment of the correlation between polymorphisms and hyperhomocysteinemia among coronary artery disease patients. I am also working on an independent project of predicting siRNA candidates as a therapeutic intervention against genes that are upregulated in acute myeloid leukemia.

Beyond the bench, I love educating people about healthcare and advocate for healthcare equality. I firmly believe that every individual should have access to top-notch healthcare, regardless of their socioeconomic status. I do this primarily as an executive of H3O Bangladesh. Currently, I am co-planning and co-organizing a Cancer Walk in Dhaka to raise awareness about breast cancer risk and encourage screening and early detection. To learn more about H3O and my work, check out the H3O page.

Outside of work, I love capturing the precious moments of my life through photographs and videos. Recently, I've embarked on a new journey of sharing these creations on my freshly opened Instagram account. If you're interested, you can catch a glimpse of my camera roll by visiting yello.its.shoily.
